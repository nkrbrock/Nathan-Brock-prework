About Me

Hello! My name is Nathan Brock, and this is my portfolio site. This portfolio is one of my very first websites using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap.I recently graduated from the University of Northwestern-St.Paul where I earned B.A.s in Linguistics, Intercultural Studies, and Biblical Studies. I live in Champlin, MN with my three cats Rose, Luna, and Ginger (all of whom are boys) along with my mom and sister. In my free time I enjoy reading, learning languages, watching Netflix and YouTube, and kayaking.

I am interested in learning more about coding and technology because I see it as a unique opportunity to help other people. Specifically, I want to learn more about software development to help Native Americans preserve and expand their languages. Statistically speaking, Native Americans are the most marginalized groups of people in our country today. By providing resources for them with technology, I am hoping that they no longer feel that they have to adopt the mainstream culture in order to feel like they can belong to the modern age.
